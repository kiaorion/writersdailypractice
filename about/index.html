About the Author

Kia Orion

Kia Orion is a novelist and the author of The Writer's Daily Practice: 365 Reflections to Build Your Writing Habit and Strengthen Your Craft. The book is an Amazon #1 bestseller in Journal Writing and Composition & Writing Skills with a 4.8 average rating and 200+ five-star reviews.

He writes psychological thrillers and crime fiction novellas, and created The Writer's Daily Practice as a tool for writers who want to build a consistent writing habit grounded in craft, not motivation.

What You Get

The Daily Email

Every morning, subscribers receive a short email with three things: a quote from a literary master, an original reflection on craft, and a writing prompt to get you on the page.

It's not a course. There's nothing to complete, no modules to work through. Just a few minutes of focused thinking about writing before you start your day. The kind of thing that compounds quietly over weeks and months.

The emails draw from the same material as the book, adapted for a daily format. Over a thousand writers receive them every morning.
